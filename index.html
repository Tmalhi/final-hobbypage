Welcome to My Hobbies Page

This website is a space where I share my favorite hobbies and how they shape who I am. From competitive sports to creative digital music and immersive gaming, these interests reflect both my personality and my skills.

Gaming

Gaming is more than just entertainment—it's a way to engage with creative stories, develop problem-solving skills, and connect with friends online. I especially enjoy:

  • FIFA – For the thrill of strategy and competition.
  • Apex Legends – To challenge myself in fast-paced tactical gameplay.
  • Minecraft – Where creativity knows no bounds.

Music

Music fuels my creativity and helps me focus. I enjoy a mix of genres—from EDM to Lo-fi and Pop. I also like making my own playlists to fit different moods or times of the day.

  • Genres: Lo-fi, EDM, Pop, Instrumentals
  • Activities: Discovering new music, creating playlists, attending live gigs

Sports

Staying active is very important to me, and sports are the perfect outlet for both fitness and fun. I play regularly with local teams and friends.

  • Football: I enjoy both watching and playing. I often play forward and midfield positions.
  • Badminton: Great for cardio and agility. I play doubles and singles matches at the community center.
  • Benefits: Improves focus, physical health, and teamwork skills.
